Project Overview

CzaryMaryOkulary.pl is an online platform dedicated to individuals seeking professional optical services and a wide range of eyewear options. The project's goal was to create a modern website that showcases the eyewear and optical services while providing users with practical information and tips related to eye care and choosing the right accessories.

  • Website

    Development of an intuitive and responsive user interface enabling easy access to sections like offers, services, and company information. The project includes a visually appealing layout for the homepage and detailed subpages.

  • Offer Presentation

    Professional presentation of services and offers. Optimized contact forms for scheduling appointments and submitting inquiries.

  • Customer Reviews Section

    Implementation of a review section where users can leave ratings and feedback. The store achieved an average rating of 5.0, reflecting a high level of customer satisfaction.

  • Quick Navigation

    Addition of intuitive navigation to the contact section and social media links, making it easy for users to access key information and share content. A Google Map was integrated to help users locate the company easily.

  • SEO Optimization

    The website was optimized for SEO to ensure better visibility in search engine results, especially for keywords related to optics and eyewear.

Unique Project Features:

  • Design:

    Unique, minimalist design highlighting the elegance and modernity of the offerings.

  • Mobile Optimization:

    The website was fully designed responsively to enable users to browse the offerings on various devices easily.

  • SEO:

    Implementation of an advanced SEO strategy for better visibility in search results.

Project Outcomes:

  • Increased website traffic and user engagement with the content, leading to higher interest in the company's offerings.
  • Building a strong online presence through social media integration and publishing valuable content.
  • Facilitating user access to the company's information and services, resulting in more inquiries and scheduled appointments.
  • Thanks to the new "Leave a Review" interface, the store experienced a growth in reviews, significantly improving customer interaction and building trust in the brand.
