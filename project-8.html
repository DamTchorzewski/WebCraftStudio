NaŚcieżce

NaŚcieżce.pl is a dedicated website for cycling enthusiasts, offering an online store, a bicycle service directory, and numerous thematic articles. As part of the collaboration, I was responsible for creating and optimizing the site, integrating with external systems, and ensuring smooth platform performance.

Project Overview

NaŚcieżce.pl combines the functionality of an online store with an informational platform for cyclists. The project aimed to create a comprehensive platform supporting both retail sales and providing essential information for cyclists. The site was designed with modern aesthetics, responsiveness, and user-friendliness in mind.

  • Online Store

    Full integration of the e-commerce system, including product and payment management.

  • Service Directory

    A list of trusted bicycle services with location details and service descriptions.

  • Articles and Advice

    Regular publications on cycling topics to engage users.

  • SEO Optimization

    High visibility in search results, resulting in an increase in organic traffic.

Unique Project Features:

  • Integrations:

    Collaboration with external payment and delivery systems.

  • Dynamic Content:

    Easy-to-manage content modules tailored to client needs.

  • User-Friendly Interface:

    Ensuring intuitive navigation and an attractive design for users.

Project Outcomes:

  • Increased sales through improved functionality of the online store.
  • Created a functional and engaging platform for cycling enthusiasts.
  • Implemented modern technologies to improve performance and accessibility.
