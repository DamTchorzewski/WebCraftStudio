CozmoBike

CozmoBike is an interactive website dedicated to cycling enthusiasts. The project involved developing an online platform that integrates e-commerce with a community space for biking activities and events. As a full-stack developer, I was responsible for designing and developing the website, optimizing its functionality, and ensuring a seamless user experience.

Project Overview

CozmoBike is a dynamic platform that combines a bike shop with an informational hub for cycling lovers. The project focuses on e-commerce, bike service directories, event listings, and expert content on cycling. I contributed to the development of the platform, ensuring the integration of key features and maintaining a responsive, user-centric design.

  • Online Store

    Integrated e-commerce functionality with product management, payments, and customer management.

  • Bike Service Directory

    Created an interactive map and list of trusted bike services, offering detailed information on each service.

  • Event Listings

    Implemented an event calendar to showcase cycling-related events and activities.

  • SEO Optimization

    Optimized the website for search engines, improving organic traffic.

Unique Project Features:

  • Multi-Feature Integration:

    Combines e-commerce, service directories, and event calendars to serve cycling enthusiasts.

  • Responsive Design:

    Ensures a seamless experience across all devices, from desktops to mobile phones.

  • User-Focused Interface:

    Prioritized intuitive navigation and ease of use for both shop customers and service users.

Project Outcomes:

  • Enhanced engagement through the interactive platform, attracting cycling fans and customers.
  • Successfully integrated various functionalities to streamline operations and provide value to users.
  • Improved performance and SEO ranking, boosting visibility for both products and services.
